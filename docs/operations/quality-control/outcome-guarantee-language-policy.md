# Outcome-Guarantee Language Policy

## Status

```text
draft
```

## Purpose

This policy defines what SearchClarity may and may not say about SEO, marketplace visibility, rankings, traffic, sales, revenue, or business outcomes.

It applies to:

- Fiverr gig titles
- Fiverr descriptions
- Fiverr FAQs
- buyer requirements
- delivery messages
- report templates
- sample reports
- SearchClarity.co pages
- social posts
- case studies
- customer messages

This is a SearchClarity quality-control policy.

It is not legal advice.

---

## Core Rule

```text
SearchClarity may diagnose, explain, recommend, and prioritize.
SearchClarity may not guarantee rankings, traffic, sales, revenue, profit, marketplace placement, or platform outcomes.
```

SearchClarity sells professional visibility analysis and recommendations.

It does not sell guaranteed outcomes.

---

## Why This Matters

SearchClarity operates in areas where outcomes depend on many factors outside SearchClarity's control:

- marketplace algorithms
- buyer demand
- competition
- product-market fit
- pricing
- product photos
- reviews
- inventory
- shipping
- seller reputation
- seasonality
- platform policy changes
- customer implementation quality

Because of this, outcome guarantees are misleading and operationally risky.

Fiverr also prohibits promises of outcomes outside the seller's control. SearchClarity must avoid any claim that implies guaranteed financial, ranking, traffic, or sales results.

---

## Forbidden Claims

Do not use phrases like:

```text
guaranteed ranking
guaranteed page 1
rank #1
rank higher guaranteed
increase your sales guaranteed
double your sales
boost traffic by X%
get X orders
make X dollars
profit guaranteed
Etsy algorithm hack
beat the algorithm
instant results
guaranteed visibility
```

Also avoid softer versions that imply the same thing:

```text
this will get you more sales
this will rank better
this will make your listing show up
this will increase traffic
this will fix your Etsy SEO
this will make Etsy favor your listing
```

---

## Approved Language

Use language like:

```text
visibility audit
diagnostic report
human-reviewed analysis
listing visibility factors
recommendations to test
prioritized action plan
keyword coverage observations
title and tag structure review
Etsy SEO observations
competitive context observations
clear next steps
implementation guidance
no ranking or sales guarantees
```

Approved phrasing examples:

| Instead of | Use |
|---|---|
| I will rank your listing higher | I will audit your listing visibility factors |
| This will increase sales | This report gives recommendations to test |
| Guaranteed Etsy SEO results | Human-reviewed Etsy SEO and visibility audit |
| I will boost your traffic | I will identify visibility gaps and improvement opportunities |
| Fix your Etsy ranking | Review title, tags, description, and keyword coverage |
| Beat your competitors | Compare visible listing patterns and identify positioning gaps |

## Required Disclaimer Pattern

Every customer-facing service page, gig description, FAQ, and report template should include a clear disclaimer.

Recommended short disclaimer:

```text
I do not guarantee specific rankings, traffic, or sales. This is a diagnostic report with human-reviewed recommendations based on visible listing factors and current marketplace best practices.
```

Recommended report disclaimer:

```text
This report is an analysis of visible listing, keyword, and marketplace factors. It provides recommendations to test, not guarantees of ranking, traffic, sales, revenue, or marketplace placement. Etsy and other platforms may change how they rank, display, or evaluate listings at any time.
```

---

## Report Recommendation Language

Major recommendations should use this structure:

```text
Observation:
Why it matters:
Recommendation:
How to implement:
Caveat:
```

Use careful verbs:

- consider
- test
- improve
- clarify
- strengthen
- reduce risk
- align with
- make easier for buyers to understand
- may help Etsy and buyers understand

Avoid absolute verbs:

- guarantee
- ensure
- force
- make Etsy rank
- get sales
- fix the algorithm
- beat everyone

- sales increases
- traffic increases

---

## Risky Customer Requests

If a buyer asks for guaranteed results, respond with a boundary statement.

Suggested response:

```text
I can review your listing's visibility factors and provide specific recommendations to test, but I cannot guarantee rankings, traffic, or sales. Etsy results depend on many factors outside any seller's control.
```

If a buyer insists on guarantees, decline the work.

---

## Trademark / IP Risk Language

Do not recommend keyword bait based on protected brands, characters, franchises, celebrities, or trademarks unless the seller clearly owns or is authorized to use them.

Approved phrasing:

```text
Avoid using brand, character, or trademark terms unless you have the right to use them. SearchClarity does not recommend trademark-risky keyword bait.
```

---

## AI / Automation Language

SearchClarity positioning should emphasize human review.

Approved:

```text
human-reviewed
analyst-reviewed
manually reviewed
clear methodology
```

Avoid implying the report is an automated dump:

```text
AI-generated keyword list
instant automated audit
bulk keyword scrape
```

## QC Checklist

Before any customer-facing asset is published or delivered:

- [ ] No ranking guarantee
- [ ] No traffic guarantee
- [ ] No sales/revenue guarantee
- [ ] No algorithm-hack language
- [ ] No trademark-risky keyword bait
- [ ] No fake certainty from directional data
- [ ] Recommendations are framed as tests or improvements
- [ ] Caveats are present
- [ ] Human-reviewed language is accurate
- [ ] Fiverr policy-sensitive language reviewed
