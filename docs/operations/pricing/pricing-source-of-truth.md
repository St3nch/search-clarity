## Pricing Principles

SearchClarity pricing should:

- avoid commodity positioning
- reflect human review time
- leave room for over-delivery on first orders
- keep the Standard tier attractive
- prevent unlimited revision/support exposure
- account for Fiverr's 20% fee
- account for report production time
- account for buyer communication and revision handling
- avoid pricing so low that buyers expect cheap keyword dumps

## Time Budget Guardrail

Approximate maximum production time targets:

| Package | Target max production time | Reason |
|---|---:|---|
| Basic | 60-90 minutes | Must stay profitable after Fiverr fee |
| Standard | 2-3 hours | Hero package with real analysis depth |
| Premium | 4-5 hours | Larger scope plus walkthrough |

If actual production time exceeds these targets repeatedly, either raise pricing, reduce scope, or split the service.

## When To Revisit Pricing

Revisit pricing when:

- first sample PDF is complete
- Fiverr UI minimum prices are verified
- first 3-5 test orders or mock orders reveal actual production time
- revision volume is higher than expected
- buyer quality is poor
- competitor pricing changes materially
- SearchClarity adds stronger proof assets
- SearchClarity moves beyond Fiverr to direct sales
