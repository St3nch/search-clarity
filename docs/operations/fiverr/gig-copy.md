## Gig Description Draft

Target: 1,000-1,100 characters. Fiverr max: 1,200 characters.

```text
Is your Etsy listing getting views but not enough traction? Or are you unsure whether your title, tags, description, and keyword targeting are helping buyers understand what you sell?

I will review your Etsy listing visibility factors and deliver a clear PDF audit with human-reviewed findings and practical recommendations.

Depending on your package, I review:
- listing title structure
- tag and keyword coverage
- description clarity
- buyer intent and search language
- visible shop/listing trust signals
- competitor/listing pattern observations
- priority action steps

You receive a professional PDF report, not a cheap keyword dump. The goal is to show what is clear, what is weak, and what changes are worth testing first.

Important: I do not guarantee specific Etsy rankings, traffic, or sales. This is a diagnostic visibility audit with recommendations based on visible listing factors and current marketplace best practices.

Order now and send your Etsy shop/listing URLs through the buyer requirements.
```

## FAQ Drafts

### FAQ 1 - What exactly will I receive?

```text
You will receive a PDF audit with findings, visibility observations, and prioritized recommendations for the Etsy listing(s) included in your package.
```

### FAQ 2 - Do you guarantee more sales or higher rankings?

```text
No. I do not guarantee rankings, traffic, or sales. This is a diagnostic audit with recommendations to test based on visible listing and marketplace factors.
```

### FAQ 3 - Do I need to share my Etsy login?

```text
No. Please do not send your Etsy password or login details. I only need your public shop/listing URLs and optional Etsy Stats screenshots if you want them considered.
```

### FAQ 4 - How many listings are included?

```text
Basic covers 1 listing, Standard covers up to 3 listings, and Premium covers up to 5 listings. Please paste the exact listing URLs in the requirements.
```

### FAQ 5 - What does the revision include?

```text
A revision covers clarification or correction within the original audit scope. It does not include reviewing new listings or implementing changes inside Etsy.
```

### FAQ 6 - Can you rewrite my title and tags too?

```text
This gig is an audit and recommendation report. Full title/tag rewriting may be offered as a separate service or custom add-on later.
```

### FAQ 7 - Do you work with digital products or POD?

```text
Yes, as long as the product is allowed on Etsy and the listing can be reviewed from public information and your optional screenshots.
```

### FAQ 8 - What if my shop is brand new?

```text
Brand-new shops can still benefit from a visibility audit. The report will focus on listing clarity, keyword coverage, and setup issues rather than past performance data.
```

Verify FAQ character limits in Fiverr UI before publishing.
