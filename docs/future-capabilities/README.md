## Candidate Workflow

Future SearchClarity candidate workflow:

```text
SearchClarity website/content need observed
-> agent summarizes evidence
-> agent proposes content or site update
-> agent drafts outline, content, metadata, schema, or internal link recommendation
-> human reviews claims, brand fit, privacy, and business value
-> approved work becomes a task or repo change
-> preview/build/checks run where relevant
-> human approves publish or merge
-> performance is monitored after publication
```

---

## Human Approval Rule

Agents may propose.

Agents may draft.

Agents may analyze.

Agents may not publish.

Agents may not merge.

Agents may not deploy.

Agents may not change pricing.

Agents may not change service promises.

Agents may not use customer/private data publicly.

Agents may not turn one noisy trend into an automatic business decision.

## Possible Agent Assistance Later

| Area | Candidate Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Website monitoring | Flag stale pages, weak internal links, broken links, or missing CTAs |
| Content ideas | Propose SearchClarity blog topics tied to services and buyer questions |
| Service page support | Suggest updates to canonical service pages and marketplace pages |
| SEO/GEO review | Check metadata, entity clarity, internal links, schema, and citation opportunities |
| Drafting support | Draft outlines or first-pass content for human review |
| Risk review | Flag unsafe claims, privacy issues, platform-policy risks, and brand problems |
| Human owner | Approve, reject, edit, publish, merge, or park the work |
